Privacy Policy

How we protect your information

Last Updated: November 2024

At EventCraft Pro, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and password when you sign up
  • Invitation Data: Event details, images, and content you create
  • Contact Information: Name and email when you contact us

Usage Information

  • How you use our platform (pages visited, features used)
  • Device information (browser type, IP address)
  • Cookies for functionality and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Create and manage your invitations
  • Send important updates about your account
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

3. Data Protection

We protect your data through:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Storage: Your files are stored on secure servers
  • Access Controls: Limited team access to user data
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and updates

4. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We only share data:

  • When you share invitations with others (as intended)
  • With service providers (hosting, analytics) under strict agreements
  • When required by law

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Edit: Update your account information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your invitations and data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails

6. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our platform
  • Improve your experience

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

7. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we'll remove your personal information within 30 days (except data we're required to keep by law).

8. Children's Privacy

EventCraft Pro is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have, we'll delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on our platform. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.

Your trust is important to us. We're committed to being transparent about how we handle your data.
