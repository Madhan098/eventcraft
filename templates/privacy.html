1. Introduction

EventCraft Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our digital invitation creation platform. By using our Service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Scope: This policy applies to all users of EventCraft Pro, including website visitors, registered users, and premium subscribers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you provide when you:

  • Create an Account: Name, email address, mobile number, and password
  • Complete Your Profile: Profile picture, location, and preferences
  • Create Invitations: Event details, guest lists, and custom content
  • Contact Support: Messages, feedback, and support requests
  • Subscribe to Premium: Payment information and billing details

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Service, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and time spent on the platform
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Log Data: Server logs, error reports, and performance metrics
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (with your consent)

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Social Media: When you connect your social media accounts
  • Payment Processors: Transaction confirmations and payment status
  • Analytics Services: Aggregated usage statistics and insights

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Service Delivery

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our digital invitation platform
  • Process your registrations and manage your account
  • Create and customize your invitations
  • Enable sharing and distribution features
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions

3.2 Communication & Support

We use your contact information to:

  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Provide customer service and technical support

3.3 Service Improvement

We analyze usage data to:

  • Improve our platform functionality and user experience
  • Develop new features and services
  • Optimize performance and fix technical issues
  • Conduct research and analytics

3.4 Legal & Security

We use your information to:

  • Comply with legal obligations and regulations
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and policies
  • Resolve disputes and legal claims

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

Important: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4.2 Limited Sharing for Service Delivery

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who help us:

  • Hosting & Infrastructure: Cloud hosting providers for data storage
  • Payment Processing: Secure payment gateways for transactions
  • Email Services: Email delivery and marketing automation
  • Analytics: Usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Customer Support: Help desk and support ticket systems

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, including:

  • Compliance with court orders or legal processes
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigation of potential violations of our policies
  • Cooperation with law enforcement agencies

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

4.5 With Your Consent

We will share your information with third parties only when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

5. Data Security & Protection

5.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Employee Training: Regular security awareness training for our team
  • Incident Response: Comprehensive incident response and recovery procedures

5.2 Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Immediately investigate and contain the breach
  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report to relevant authorities as required by law
  • Implement additional security measures to prevent future breaches

5.3 Your Role in Security

You can help protect your information by:

  • Using strong, unique passwords for your account
  • Keeping your login credentials confidential
  • Logging out when using shared devices
  • Regularly updating your contact information

6. Data Retention & Deletion

6.1 Retention Periods

We retain your information for different periods based on the type of data:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active
  • Invitation Content: Retained for 2 years after creation
  • Usage Data: Retained for 1 year for analytics purposes
  • Payment Information: Retained as required by financial regulations
  • Support Communications: Retained for 3 years for service quality

6.2 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data through:

  • Account Settings: Delete your account and associated data
  • Support Request: Contact our support team for data deletion
  • Email Request: Send deletion requests to privacy@eventcraftpro.com

6.3 Deletion Process

When you request data deletion, we will:

  • Verify your identity before processing the request
  • Delete your personal information within 30 days
  • Retain certain information as required by law
  • Provide confirmation of deletion completion
