Account Policy

Your rights and responsibilities

Account Creation and Management

By creating an account with EventCraft Pro, you agree to provide accurate, current, and complete information. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and for all activities that occur under your account.

Account Requirements

  • You must be at least 13 years old to create an account
  • You must provide a valid email address
  • You are responsible for keeping your account information up to date
  • You must not share your account credentials with others

Account Security

We take account security seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your account information. However, you are also responsible for:

  • Using a strong, unique password
  • Not sharing your login credentials
  • Logging out when using shared devices
  • Reporting any unauthorized access immediately

Account Termination

You may delete your account at any time through your account settings. Upon account deletion:

  • All your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Your invitations will be removed from our servers
  • You will lose access to all your created invitations
  • This action cannot be undone

Content Ownership

You retain full ownership of all content you create, including invitations, images, and text. By using our service, you grant us a limited license to:

  • Store and display your content on our platform
  • Process your content to provide our services
  • Create backups of your content

Account Restrictions

You agree not to:

  • Create multiple accounts to circumvent restrictions
  • Use automated tools to access our services
  • Share or sell your account to others
  • Use your account for illegal or unauthorized purposes
  • Violate any applicable laws or regulations

Data and Privacy

Your account data is handled according to our Privacy Policy. We collect and process only the information necessary to provide our services. You can access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through your account settings.

Service Availability

We strive to maintain 99.9% uptime, but we do not guarantee uninterrupted access to our services. We reserve the right to:

  • Perform scheduled maintenance
  • Suspend accounts that violate our terms
  • Modify or discontinue features with notice

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Account Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through our platform. Continued use of your account after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
