Everything you need to know about creating beautiful invitations

How do I create an invitation?

Simply choose a template, customize it with your event details, add photos, and share it with your guests. It takes less than 5 minutes!

Is it free to use?

Yes! Creating and sharing invitations is completely free. No credit card required.

Can I customize the invitation design?

Absolutely! You can customize colors, fonts, add photos, and personalize every detail to match your event style.

How do guests RSVP?

Guests can RSVP directly from the invitation link. They can respond via mobile number or email, and you'll receive all responses in your dashboard.

Can I track who viewed my invitation?

Yes! Our analytics dashboard shows you how many people viewed your invitation, RSVP responses, and more insights.

Do invitations work on mobile devices?

Yes! All invitations are fully responsive and look beautiful on phones, tablets, and desktops.
