Getting Started

Creating your first invitation is easy! Follow these simple steps:

  1. Click "Create New Invitation" on the homepage
  2. Choose a template that matches your event type
  3. Fill in your event details using our step-by-step wizard
  4. Preview your invitation and make any final adjustments
  5. Share your invitation with guests

You can have a beautiful invitation ready in just a few minutes!

EventCraft Pro supports a wide variety of events including:

  • Weddings - Traditional, modern, and cultural ceremonies
  • Birthday Parties - All ages and themes
  • Anniversaries - Milestone celebrations
  • Baby Showers - Gender reveals and celebrations
  • Graduation Parties - Academic achievements
  • Retirement Parties - Career milestones
  • Corporate Events - Business gatherings
  • Holiday Parties - Seasonal celebrations

Each event type has specialized templates designed for that occasion.

RSVP Management

Our RSVP system makes guest management easy:

  1. Add Guests - Manually add guests or import from a list
  2. Send Invitations - Share your invitation link with guests
  3. Track Responses - View real-time RSVP status updates
  4. Manage Details - Track plus-ones, dietary requirements, and messages
  5. Send Reminders - Automatically remind guests who haven't responded

Guests can respond directly through your invitation link, and you'll see updates instantly in your dashboard.

Yes! You can send reminder emails in several ways:

  • Automatic Reminders - Set up automatic reminders for guests who haven't responded
  • Manual Reminders - Send individual or bulk reminder emails
  • Custom Messages - Personalize reminder messages
  • Scheduled Reminders - Set specific dates for reminder emails

Reminder emails include a direct link to your invitation and RSVP form.

To export your guest list:

  1. Go to your invitation's RSVP management page
  2. Click the "Export" button in the top right
  3. Choose your export format (CSV, Excel, or PDF)
  4. Select which information to include (names, emails, RSVP status, etc.)
  5. Download your file

Exported files include all guest information, RSVP responses, and any additional notes or dietary requirements.

Analytics & Insights

Our analytics dashboard provides comprehensive insights:

  • View Statistics - Total views, unique visitors, and viewing patterns
  • RSVP Analytics - Response rates, attendance breakdown, and trends
  • Device Analytics - How guests are viewing your invitation (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Share Analytics - Which sharing methods are most effective
  • Geographic Data - Where your guests are located
  • Time Analytics - Peak viewing times and engagement patterns

Use these insights to optimize your invitation strategy and improve engagement.

Based on analytics data, here are tips to improve performance:

  • Optimize for Mobile - Most guests view invitations on mobile devices
  • Share at Peak Times - Send invitations during high-engagement hours
  • Use Multiple Channels - Share via email, WhatsApp, and social media
  • Send Reminders - Follow up with guests who haven't responded
  • Test Different Templates - A/B test different designs to see what works
  • Personalize Messages - Add personal touches to increase engagement
