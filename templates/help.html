Frequently Asked Questions

Is EventCraft Pro free to use?
Yes! EventCraft Pro is free to use. You can create, customize, and share unlimited invitations at no cost.
Do my guests need an account to view invitations?
No! Your guests can view and RSVP to invitations without creating an account. They just need the link you share.
Can I upload my own photos?
Absolutely! You can upload your own photos to personalize your invitations. We support JPG, PNG, and most common image formats.
How do I share my invitation?
After creating your invitation, you'll get a unique link. Share this link via WhatsApp, email, SMS, or social media. You can also generate a QR code for easy sharing.
Can I edit my invitation after creating it?
Yes! You can edit your invitations anytime from your dashboard. Changes are reflected immediately for everyone with the link.
What events can I create invitations for?
Weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, baby showers, graduations, retirements, corporate events, and more! We have templates for all occasions.
Are my invitations secure?
Yes! Your data is encrypted and stored securely. Only people with your invitation link can view it.
Can I track RSVPs?
Yes! We provide built-in RSVP tracking. You can see who's responded and manage your guest list easily.
Does it work on mobile phones?
Absolutely! EventCraft Pro works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Your invitations look great on all screen sizes.
How long do invitations stay active?
Your invitations remain active for 1 year by default. You can delete them anytime or extend the expiration if needed.
